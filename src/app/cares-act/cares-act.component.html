CARES Act Details

What is the CARES Act?
The Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act (CARES Act) is a $2 trillion stimulus allocated for businesses, individuals, federal agencies, and state and local governments to assist in relieving stress and uncertainty being experienced during the coronavirus pandemic. The CARES Act is designed to distribute capital quickly and broadly, with many provisions to help small businesses. Read the bill here: CARES Act
